Why-PEB

Strength: Steel is 18 times stronger than concrete and therefore steel constructions arc stronger as dead weight is lesser, Strength is also assured by design. Stability in the qualities of steel makes it possible to innovate stronger and better designs. Steel can hold 6 times its own weight and can handle equipment like multiple cranes and sways can be controlled.



Cost Saving: There is 12-50 percent cost saving in long-span steel buildings. As it is, erection cost is low at the site. Manpower required is lesser therefore the labor cost for construction is also low.

Timelines: The building can be designed and erected in lesser time as work can be done simultaneously. Since there is virtually no idle time, sundaram PEBs can be completed approximately 50 percent of the time as compared to concrete buildings.

Scalability and Modifications: Sled buildings can be easily expanded or modified as compared to concrete buildings. Last moment and post erection modifications are also possible by cutting, welding, and attaching steel parts. More bays can be added by connecting the secondary members and the sheeting to the old steel construction.


Site: As the buildings are fabricated in the factory, construction time at the site is reduced by more than 75 percent.


Maintenance: Maintenance and cost of repair in case of steel building is much lesser.


Safety: SUNDARAM PEBs can take on massive shocks and earthquakes due to the ductility of steel. Steel shows signs of failure and gives chance to repair thereby making the building safer in case of natural calamity or an accident. Light steel structure minimizes the seismic effect on the structure.


Workmanship: Accuracy and tolerances in the case of PEBs are much finer as compared to concrete buildings. As members are fabricated in a controlled environment, fabrication is precise.


Logistics: Steel buildings can be delivered anywhere in the world. This leads to capital investment saving.
